Admission Formalities

Once your child's name is included in the admission list, kindly adhere to the following formalities:


1. Fee Payment: Pay the required fee by the indicated dates mentioned in the admission list to secure admission. Failure to do so will result in automatic cancellation of admission.


2. Submission of Forms: Carefully fill and submit the Admission Form, Transportation Form, and Medical Form along with the fee payment.


3. Document Verification: Ensure that the original Birth Certificate (for Nursery & Prep) and Transfer Certificate (for Classes 1 and above) are submitted for verification.


4. Issuance of Student ID Card: The student ID card will be issued upon completion of all forms and formalities.


5. Reporting Instructions:

  • • Report to the Reception Counter of the school on the specified dates mentioned in the joining instructions.

  • • Ensure that your child reports to school wearing the designated uniform and carries the prescribed books and stationery.

6. Session Commencement:


  • • Admissions typically commence at the beginning of the academic session, i.e., in the month of April.

  • • Transfer students must provide a School Leaving Certificate from the previous institution.

Refund and Withdrawal Policies


  • • Fee Refund: Fees once paid are non-refundable under any circumstances.

  • • Withdrawal Procedure: Submit a withdrawal application on the prescribed proforma available at the School Office. A one-month notice period is required for withdrawal, and clearance from various departments is necessary before obtaining the Transfer Certificate.
