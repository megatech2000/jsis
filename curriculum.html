Curriculum philosophy

JSIS is a school that follows an English medium, co-educational approach. We are in the process of seeking affiliation with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) by the upcoming year. With English as the medium of instruction, our institution is dedicated to providing comprehensive education to every student.


Our school advocates for an integrated curriculum system, where units and topics are taught across various disciplines using specific methods. This approach allows our students to make connections across different subjects. Activities, projects, presentations, and educational trips are integral components of our curriculum, aimed at ensuring strong and enduring concept formation. Additionally, we organize themed weeks every month, such as environment, literary, cultural, and science weeks, aligning with the syllabus prescribed by CBSE.


Co-curricular activities are essential elements of our curriculum, offering students a competitive edge and fostering personality development. We offer a diverse range of activities, including Western and Indian music, dance, art and craft activities, and clay modelling. The curriculum aims to cultivate application, analytical, and creative skills among our students.


For our youngest learners, we adopt a play-way method of teaching, providing individualized attention to make learning enjoyable. Our classes are theme-designed to facilitate learning through observation, hands-on experiences, and the application of knowledge. We utilize audio-visual aids and practical demonstrations extensively to ensure that learning is engaging and enriching. Additionally, we provide student counselling at all levels to address any special needs or challenges that students may encounter.
