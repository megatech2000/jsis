Facilities

Jnanasahyadri International Schools prioritizes holistic student development, offering abundant opportunities for comprehensive growth. JISS is dedicated to achieving remarkable progress, boasting top-tier infrastructure, a scenic campus, and state-of-the-art facilities.


Location: Nestled amidst the serene backdrop of the enchanting Western Ghats, JISS is renowned for its stunning architecture and verdant surroundings.


Building: The architecture of JISS, Soraba is thoughtfully crafted to exude a contemporary, chic, and global appeal. Situated within a vast 10-acre campus, the school building stands as an impressive structure.


Classrooms: Equipped with interactive features like LCD projectors, whiteboards, and flannel boards, JISS classrooms provide an optimal environment for academic advancement. They are spacious and well-ventilated, ensuring a conducive learning atmosphere.


Student Support: In a commitment to enhancing student comprehension, teachers allocate additional hours within the regular school schedule. During these sessions, students benefit from personalized interaction with teachers, fostering a thorough understanding of subjects and addressing any queries they may have.


JSIS offers a diverse range of sports facilities to cater to the holistic development and recreational needs of its students. From athletics to yoga, martial arts to volleyball, throw ball to kabaddi, kho-kho to roller-skating, cricket, football,


handball and many more, our school provides a comprehensive array of options for students to explore and engage in physical activities. Whether it's individual pursuits like yoga and martial arts or team sports such as football, cricket, basketball, and hockey, JSIS ensures that students have access to a variety of sports to suit their interests and preferences. Additionally, facilities for less conventional sports like ultimate Frisbee are also available, providing students with opportunities to discover new and exciting activities. With well-equipped facilities and dedicated coaches, JSIS promotes a culture of sportsmanship, teamwork, and physical fitness among its students, encouraging them to excel both on and off the field.


Mathematics Laboratory


In the Math Lab, students engage with mathematical concepts through interactive games, puzzles, and modern teaching aids. Apart from guided sessions, students are encouraged to utilize the lab independently, fostering a sense of exploration and innovation.


Social Science Laboratory


Equipped with maps, globes, and ethnographic artefacts, the Social Science Laboratory facilitates both practical and theoretical learning. It serves as a collaborative space for students and teachers to engage in interactive activities like role-playing, visualization, problem-solving, and group discussions.


Green School Initiatives


We are dedicated to instilling the principles of sustainability among our students and faculty. Through hands-on experiences with solar cells and energy storage, as well as projects focused on recycling and waste management, students actively explore environmental concepts.


Computer Laboratory


Our Computer lab is equipped with high-speed internet and the latest educational software, prioritizing security and safety. Each student has access to a terminal, and we emphasize personal safety in social media usage to ensure


awareness of associated risks. Parents can track their children's progress in each subject through the school's website.


Language Laboratory


Featuring state-of-the-art multimedia equipment, our Language Laboratory facilitates language learning through interactive sessions, recording capabilities, and practice exercises. Students improve their language skills in an engaging environment.


Music and Dance


Students are provided with opportunities for self-expression through music and dance. Those interested in formal training receive additional support outside the regular curriculum and showcase their talents at annual school events.


Co-curricular Activities


Our students participate in various social activities to learn about diverse communities and promote universal tolerance. Examples include sharing knowledge with differently-abled or minority community peers and tutoring academically weaker classmates.


Art & Craft


In addition to drawing and painting, students explore a variety of crafts such as pottery, handmade paper, and textile arts. Our art facilities draw inspiration from Indian traditions, providing students with rich cultural experiences.
